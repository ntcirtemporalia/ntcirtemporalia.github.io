Home


Welcome to the home page of NTCIR Temporal Information Access (Temporalia) Task. Temporalia is a new pilot task accepted to run at NTCIR-11. Please subscribe to our discussion group if you're interested in the task.


Please contact us for informal enquiries: tc4fia at googlegroups dot com.


Paper submission deadline: September 15th, 2014 (Hawaii Time)
Camera-Ready Paper submission deadline: November 1st, 2014 (Hawaii Time)


Temporalia is also described in the recent publication:


Hideo Joho, Adam Jatowt, and Roi Blanco: NTCIR Temporalia: A Test Collection for Temporal Information Access Research, Proceedings of the 4th Temporal Web Analytics Workshop (TempWeb 2014), ACM Press, Seoul, Korea, pp. 845-849.
